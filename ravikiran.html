About Me

My name is Sagi Ravi Kiran, and I am from Garividi, Vizianagaram district, Andhra Pradesh. My father, Bhaskara Raju, is a private employee, and my mother, Hymavathi, is a homemaker. I have an older brother who runs Finjoy Investments, a company specializing in stock market investments.

I completed my SSC at Siv Sai High School in 2017 with a GPA of 8.8. Following my SSC, I achieved a rank of 1896 in the POLYCET exam and pursued Mechanical Engineering at Government Polytechnic, Visakhapatnam.

During my polytechnic years, I served as a Class Representative (CR) for three years, strengthening my leadership skills. In my final semester, I undertook an industrial training program at RINL Steel Plant, Visakhapatnam.

Post-polytechnic, I gained professional experience at Tata Authorized Service Center and later worked as a Junior Engineer at Pokarna Engineered Stone Limited. However, my passion for technology led me to pursue a B.Tech in Computer Science and Data Science at Raghu Institute of Technology.

Initially, transitioning from a mechanical background to computer science was challenging. However, through consistent effort, I gradually improved my technical skills. I was fortunate to be selected for an advanced DSA training program conducted in collaboration with Smart Interviews, which greatly enhanced my problem-solving abilities.
