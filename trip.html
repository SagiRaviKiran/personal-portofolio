Trips | Sagi Ravi Kiran

Araku Trip

A thrilling adventure with my entire class! We embarked on a lively bus journey filled with music, dance, and laughter. The mesmerizing waterfalls welcomed us for a refreshing dip, followed by an exploration of lush coffee plantations and serene pine forests. As the night set in, we gathered around a campfire, sharing stories and swaying to music. The next morning, we marveled at the stunning Madagada viewpoint before venturing into the mystical Burra Caves. The adrenaline rush of zip-lining near the caves was unforgettable! The trip ended on a high note as we danced our way back home, cherishing every memory made.

Odisha Trip

A delightful road trip with my cousins Teja, Hari, Sanvi, and Sanju! Our first stop was Bhubaneswar, where we savored the iconic Chennapoda sweet. The night was spent in a beautifully decorated hotel, capturing moments in stunning photographs. The journey continued as we explored the grandeur of Lingaraj Temple, followed by the awe-inspiring Konark Sun Temple, a masterpiece of ancient architecture. A serene evening at Puri Beach set the perfect mood before an unexpected minor accident added a twist to our adventure. Nonetheless, the highlight was visiting the sacred Jagannath Temple, leaving us with a heart full of memories as we headed back home.

Kakinada Trip

An exhilarating road trip with Kusuma, Lokesh, and Dushyanth! Our first stop was the enchanting Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary, where we embraced nature at its finest, capturing mesmerizing shots along the way. The highlight of the trip was the breathtaking drive along Uppada Beach Road, where the sea breeze and scenic views left us in awe. The tranquil beach provided the perfect setting for relaxation before we made our way back home, filled with joy and countless unforgettable moments.
